Big heart and big talent

The story began back in 2015. The big idea was to acquire uniquely talented people with expertise in consultation and solution development and encourage them to do what they do best. The main requirements: big heart and big talent.

In a nutshell, our consultants create solutions and tools. Through investigation of our clients' needs, we gather existing materials and tailor solutions that add direct value to their services and company growth. This value improves productivity, which in turn increases profits. We create custom products and solutions using the development tools of your choice. We reinforce these products with our expertise to meet business performance goals. Our clients take advantage of our talents in the design and development of solutions, support and much more. In our experience, a well cared for client awards additional projects.

Customer relationship

At Siria we focus on long-term customer relationships. Our business is built upon industry knowledge and a deep understanding of our customers’ core processes, technology know-how and on our own products embedded to our services. Siaira is building its future growth by investing in CSI capabilities, utilizing its cross-selling opportunities in the emerging economies like India and Africa by bringing new solutions with innovative offering.
Siaria’s offering has been developed to meet all of our clients’ IT challenges. Our integrated service offering combines Managed Services, Consulting and System Integration that are embedded into each Industry offering. With this service structure, we are able to provide full life-cycle IT services. We also focus on developing our services and products within our Product Development Services business area that continues to differentiate itself by combining its deep customer process knowledge with technical expertise.

Siaria has partnered with IT services company in different geographical location and with professionals in many countries who are working on the frontline of a wide range of industries. We are not an organisation with a complex hierarchy; instead we maintain our high quality by learning and improving as a team, and taking every new challenge as an opportunity to grow –as a team.

Company Moto

It’s about being curious. It’s about collaboration. It’s about seeing what’s coming next. And we act and live by this simple motto: when our customer succeeds, we succeed.
